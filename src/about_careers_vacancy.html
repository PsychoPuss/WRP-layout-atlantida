STRATEGIC LAND MANAGER

PERM / SALARY: TBC / EAST MOLESEY – LONDON

A new position within the land team – initially the role will entail liaising with the Board and Land Director to understand and align with the company land requirements, to formulate and produce a structure, plan and budgets as a foundation for the management of a system for the future growth of a pipeline of strategic land opportunities. Primarily, this will be by making direct approaches to land owners, although existing contacts will also need to be managed to ensure suitable strategic land is considered.

The role is to bolster the existing land team and as such, to be involved in every aspect of bringing new strategic land opportunities forward for consideration, evaluating them, putting forward offer proposals, agreeing and managing legal contracts, reporting to the main Board for approval and subsequently, managing all planning and technical matters to facilitate bringing sites forward for development.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

Setting up systems and procedures in collaboration with the Land Director & Head of Planning for the satisfactory management of strategic land opportunities

Generating new strategic land opportunities (direct approaches)

Generating new strategic land opportunities (agent contacts)

Fielding land enquiries, qualifying and responding accordingly

Visiting sites/land owners for the purposes of assessing their suitability and viability

Negotiating, agreeing and progressing deals to contract

Managing site opportunities and contracted sites through planning

Collating & filing information (planning/technical/legal, development, etc.) for new sites

Liaising with all teams across the group and externally with land owners, agents and consultants

Producing reports for strategic land acquisition approval

REQUIREMENTS:

A good planning background

Excellent organisational, time management and investigative skills

Good IT Skills eg. Excel, Word, PowerPoint

Self-motivated and proactive, with the ability to meet deadlines

Able to identify and solve issues that arise

Excellent verbal and written communication

Working effectively within and across all Octagon teams

A driving license and car for site visits
